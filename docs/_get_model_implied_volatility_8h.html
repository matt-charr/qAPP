Reprice the list of vanilla options written on a specific Equity/FX underlying with a specific maturity date and a list of strikes provided by the user. Assess the ability for the model to match the market implied volatilities.

Warning
User-provided market data is restricted to premium users only.
By default, your market will be defined according to data/my_data_file.json.
Any modification to this file will not be effective.
